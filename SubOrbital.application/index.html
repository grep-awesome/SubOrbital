FAQ

  • What do you mean it can screw up my system?
    • SubOrbital does its thing by editing files in "/var/luna/preferences/launcher3". Those files are key to the Launcher on webOS to work correctly. If SubOrbital goes postal, it will not brick your device, but it can prevent it from working until you hash out those files. My strong suggestion is to back them up.
  • Why can't I edit the default tabs?
    • The launcher doesn't really respect its config files. It only partially respects them. To know the real answer, you'd have to ask the webOS guys.
  • Why doesn't my tab save / what characters are valid?
    • If you try to name a tab with anything other than alpha-numberi characters (also - and _), it will not save.
  • I got an error Dialog! What now?
    • Error dialogs here are a good thing. It means SubOrbital caught the error and likely didn't do damage. On the other hand SubOrbital probably didn't do what you asked it to either. An error log will be placed in /media/internal/SubOrbital_error/. If you'd kindly send that to me on precentral (hiddenpremise) or post it on the official thread for the app, I'll try to track down and fix the issue.
